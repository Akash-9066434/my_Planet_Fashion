Custom Tailoring and Alterations

About Our Custom Tailoring Service

At Planet Fashion, we offer professional tailoring services to ensure your clothing fits perfectly and reflects your personal style. Whether you need minor alterations or custom-made garments, our experienced tailors will work with you to achieve the perfect fit and design.

Key Features of the Service

  • **Made-to-Measure Garments**: Create custom clothing tailored to your exact measurements and preferences.
  • **High-Quality Fabrics**: Choose from a wide range of premium fabrics to suit your taste and budget.
  • **Alterations**: From simple hemming to more complex alterations, we make sure your clothes fit just right.
  • **Custom Designs**: Work with our designers to create unique garments that reflect your personal style, whether it’s for everyday wear, special occasions, or professional attire.
  • **Restyling Old Garments**: Give new life to your existing wardrobe with expert restyling and modification services.
  • **Perfect Fit Guarantee**: We ensure that your tailored garments fit perfectly, with multiple fittings if necessary.

How It Works

The custom tailoring process is straightforward and designed to meet your specific needs:

  1. **Consultation**: Meet with one of our tailors to discuss your garment requirements, fabric selection, and design preferences.
  2. **Measurement**: Our tailors will take detailed measurements to ensure a perfect fit.
  3. **Fittings**: We offer one or more fittings to ensure the garment fits comfortably and meets your expectations.
  4. **Alterations**: If you already have garments that need adjustments, bring them in, and we’ll tailor them to fit you perfectly.
  5. **Final Delivery**: Once completed, your custom-tailored garment will be ready for collection or delivery.
